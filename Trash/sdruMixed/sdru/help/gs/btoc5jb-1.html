Hardware and Firmware

The blocks and System objects use a specific version of the UHDTM driver. The FPGA binaries on your USRP® radio must match the specific version of the UHDTM drivers supported by the current release.

For a list of currently required and supported hardware and firmware, refer to the most recent Release Notes.
